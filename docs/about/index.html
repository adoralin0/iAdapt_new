About Us

Leading adaptation and resilient planning and design in response to climate change and sea level rise.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become the preeminent research and education center for adaptation and resilient planning and design in response to climate change and sea level rise.

Our Mission

To study the impacts of natural hazards and develop adaptive strategies in response to climate change and other natural hazards from the empirical and theoretical perspectives.

To provide guidance to coastal communities to take proactive adaptation actions to increase their resiliency.
